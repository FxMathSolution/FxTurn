How Does FxTurn Trade Fusion AI Work?

FxTurn Trade Fusion AI operates in three simple yet powerful stages:

  • 1

    Signal Generation

    The AI/ML models analyze vast amounts of market data in real-time to identify high-probability trading opportunities. These models are trained to recognize patterns, trends, and anomalies, ensuring that every signal is backed by data-driven insights.

  • 2

    Trade Execution

    Once a signal is generated, the EA automatically executes trades on your behalf. You can choose to trade all 20 supported currency pairs (basket trading) or focus on specific pairs. This flexibility allows you to tailor your trading strategy to your preferences and risk tolerance.

  • 3

    Profit Management

    The EA is designed to maximize profits while minimizing risk. It automatically closes all open positions when the total profit across all trades reaches your predefined target. This disciplined approach ensures that you lock in gains and avoid emotional decision-making.
